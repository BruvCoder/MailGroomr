This is the Mail Groomr extension.

Mail Groomr is a Chrome extension that helps you quickly clean up old emails in your Gmail account by selecting a date range.

Mail Groomr connects securely to your Gmail account via Google's official OAuth system. Your data is never stored, shared, or transmitted outside of your Gmail account.
