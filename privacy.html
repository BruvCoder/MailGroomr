Privacy Policy

Mail Groomr uses access to your Gmail account solely for the purpose of identifying and deleting emails that match the date range you provide.

No data is collected, stored, or shared outside your own Gmail account. The extension does not transmit your email contents or personal information to any external servers.

All actions (such as moving emails to trash) occur directly within your Gmail account via the official Gmail API.
